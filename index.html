Core Drivers & Inhibitors
The strongest positive driver is Schooling (r = 0.82), which indicates developmental education significantly promotes longevity. In contrast, HIV/AIDS has a profound negative correlation (r = -0.56) impacting mortality rates in developing subregions.
Socio-Economic Trajectories
GDP and Income composition represent the core wealth barriers. Developing countries show a steeper slope of improvement than developed countries once schooling rises above 10 years, representing a critical developmental threshold.
Strategic Health Policies
Based on predictive weights, shifting educational access by just +2 years yields a larger projected growth in life expectancy (+1.6 years) than a 50% increase in GDP in low-income brackets, outlining education as the highest ROI public policy.

About This Platform

Portfolio project architecture and technical stack overview.

The Global Life Expectancy Intelligence Platform is a premium data science portfolio application engineered to demonstrate high-level business intelligence dashboard development, automated statistical insights, and in-browser predictive modeling.

Statistical Core: Preprocessed using Python (Pandas, Statsmodels) to clean columns, identify missing data patterns (imputed with medians), resolve boxplot outliers, and train an OLS Multiple Linear Regression model (R² = 78.7%) for live simulations.

Frontend Technology: Structured entirely in Vanilla ES6 JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3 custom grid systems. Spatial visualization is powered by Leaflet.js rendering geographic choropleths. Interactive plotting, regression paths, histograms, and boxplots are rendered using Plotly.js. No styling frameworks (e.g. TailwindCSS, Bootstrap) or JS frameworks (e.g. React, Vue) were loaded, resulting in rapid load speeds, clean styling, and full offline accessibility.
